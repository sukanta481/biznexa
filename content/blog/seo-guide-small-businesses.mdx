## Why SEO Matters More Than Ever

In 2025, 68% of all online experiences begin with a search engine. If your business isn't showing up on page 1 of Google, you're invisible to the majority of potential customers.

## The Foundation: Technical SEO

Before creating any content, ensure your website's technical foundation is solid:

### Site Speed

Google has confirmed that page speed is a ranking factor. Aim for:
- **Largest Contentful Paint (LCP):** Under 2.5 seconds
- **First Input Delay (FID):** Under 100 milliseconds
- **Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS):** Under 0.1

### Mobile-First Design

With over 60% of searches happening on mobile, your website must be responsive. Google uses mobile-first indexing — your mobile site IS your primary site.

### Schema Markup

Structured data helps Google understand your content. Implement:
- **Organization** schema for your business
- **LocalBusiness** schema for local SEO
- **FAQ** schema for question-based content
- **Article** schema for blog posts

## Content Strategy

### Keyword Research

Focus on long-tail keywords with clear intent. Use tools like:
- Google Keyword Planner
- Ahrefs
- SEMrush

### Content Calendar

Consistency is key. Publish at least 2-4 high-quality articles per month targeting different stages of the buyer journey.

## Local SEO

For businesses serving a specific area:

1. **Claim your Google Business Profile** — This is non-negotiable
2. **Get reviews** — Respond to every review, positive or negative
3. **Local citations** — Ensure NAP consistency across directories
4. **Create location-specific content** — Target "service + city" keywords

## Measuring Success

Track these metrics monthly:
- Organic traffic growth
- Keyword rankings
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate from organic traffic
- Domain authority
